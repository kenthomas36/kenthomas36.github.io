You stand before the entrance to the labyrinth

You have tread upon a path that will never be untrodden again.
A voice beckons you from the dark.
"Weary Traveller, to enter this labyrinth you must enable yourself to see beyond the material plane.
You know of what I speak, or you would never have found this place. You know of the cracks that exist in this world."
From the dark, you hear the hack of a sharp blade piercing a soft surface. As the voice returns, you hear it chewing while talking.
"The password is written on the brown earth"
